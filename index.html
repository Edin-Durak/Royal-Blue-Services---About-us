Our story

Royal Blue Services was founded in 2019 and aims to offer our clients top of the line staff at more affordable rates than they currently pay. We work with companies based out of United States who need customer service, sales, IT, or any other type of services that can be performed via computer or phone. Using our proven recruiting, qualifying, vetting, training, and managing process, we provide a strong workforce in Bosnia and Herzegovina to fulfill your needs. The staff we hire works for Royal Blue Services, but acts on your behalf. You will have full access to training and evaluating their performance.

Royal Blue Services provides the candidates and helps you select and train your team. We also provide the equipment, office space, and handle local labor laws. We remain your trusted partner in business, while allowing you to train the staff as if they were working next to you in your own office. Our mission is to find the best talent available and help them find the best companies to work for. This way, both the employee and employer is happy. The local, Bosnian will have access to better working conditions, better pay, and a better future, while the United States based companies benefit from a more motivated workforce at a fraction of the cost they are used to paying.
